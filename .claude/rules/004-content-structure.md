## Page Purposes

### Homepage (7 sections)
1. **Hero** — H1 + value proposition + 2 CTAs + context note (800.000M€)
2. **Context ("Por qué ahora")** — European/Spanish defense market data
3. **Audience ("Para quién es DUALYS")** — 3 cards: industriales, tech, primes
4. **Key Messages** — 5 messages reinforcing authority
5. **Verticals** — Visual summary of 9 defense verticals (no weapons)
6. **Team Trust** — Founding team description (anonymous, 4 profiles)
7. **Final CTA** — Dark background, "Construir capacidades duales para la industria"

### Servicios
- Program overview (enigmatic — don't reveal full methodology)
- Branded as "Programa FFD (FIT FOR DEFENSE)" or "La Ruta Dual Use"
- CTA to contact

### Sectores
- 9 defense verticals with company profile fit and transferable capabilities
- "De dónde vienes → a dónde puedes ir" section
- No weapons/ammo references

### Metodología
- "Programa FFD" branded methodology (enigmatic)
- 3 phases: Diagnóstico → Posicionamiento → Activación
- NDA/confidentiality mention
- CTA to contact

### Nosotros
- Company narrative ("DUALYS nace de una convicción...")
- 4 anonymous founder profiles
- Vision timeline: El Despertar → La Aceleración → El Liderazgo
- CTA to contact

### Recursos / Insights
- Categories: Análisis de mercado, Guías y tutoriales, Casos de transición, Opinión y tendencias
- Minimum 5-8 articles before launch

### Contact
- Form: nombre, empresa, email, sector (dropdown), descripción del reto
- Address: Camí Can Ametller 36 Planta Baixa, 08195 Sant Cugat del Vallès
- Email: info@dualys.eu
- RGPD consent checkbox
